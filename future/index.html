About the bakery

Handcrafted bakes, heartfelt celebrations, and plenty of butter—all coming together in our cozy ATL kitchen.

A love letter to cookies

Every Barely Berndt creation starts with a childhood memory: the warm cookie cake at the center of every party. We remix nostalgic favorites with brown butter, flaky salt, and unexpected sprinkles.

Small batch, big heart

We keep our batches intimate so each crumb gets attention. From hand-painted icing to richly layered dough, you'll taste the care (and the butter) in every bite.

Celebrations of all sizes

Cookie cakes, sprinkle-lined bars, and party-ready treat boxes make it easy to celebrate everything from Tuesday wins to milestone birthdays.

Cookie cake & bite menus

What’s a cookie bite?

Bite size

Each cookie bite is roughly 2" wide—the perfect one- or two-bite treat, available decorated or plain with a side of buttercream.

Easy peasy

No slicing required. They’re party-ready and ideal for boosting the serving count of a large cookie cake.

Cute AF

Dress them to match your decor or keep them simple and classic—either way, they turn heads.

Ordering timeline

We bake to order and keep things cozy. Here's how to reserve your treat and make sure it's perfect.

Dream up your dessert

Peek at the menu, pull inspiration from your favorite celebrations, and gather any must-have details or dietary notes.

Send a request

Drop us a line with your date, portion needs, and any custom decoration ideas. We'll confirm availability within 48 hours.

Lock in the bake

Once we align on plan and pricing, we'll secure your spot on the baking calendar with a 25% deposit.

Pickup or delivery

Choose local pickup from our Kirkwood kitchen or arrange an in-town delivery. We'll package everything to arrive party ready.
